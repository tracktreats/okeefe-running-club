Overview

About the Team

We practice twice a week, accommodate all ability levels, and compete in track meets across the spring season: focused on the 400m, 1 mile, and 2 mile.

Practice Days

Mondays & Wednesdays before school

Arrival Time

7:40 am: warm-ups start promptly at 7:45 am, done by ~8:45 am

Where to Meet

Meet in the gym before practice. Enter through the back door near the playground off Spaight St.

Pace Groups

We form groups by pace & distance so every skill level is supported

Season Starts

Monday, March 30: right after spring break!

Events

400m, 1 mile, and 2 mile: spring 2026 season

Cost

No cost to families for meets: fees are covered by the team

Multi-sport athletes are very welcome: we understand there will be schedule conflicts with other spring sports and work around them.

Volunteers needed!

As the club has grown, we could really use 1–2 additional adults out on the streets with the kids during practice. You can walk at the back as a sweep (great for kids not yet ready to run the full practice), or run/bike with a pace group. Even one morning a week makes a huge difference.

Interested? Reach out to the coaches directly: contact info below.

Getting Started

How to Join

Three steps to get your kid set up on the team.

1

Add your kid to the team roster

Fill in a row in the team roster & meet attendance Google Sheet with your child's full name, grade, gender (two columns: see sheet for details), and birthdate. Also include a parent/guardian contact name and phone number. The coaches will share the sheet link via WhatsApp (step 2).

2

Join the WhatsApp community

This is the primary way coaches communicate with parents: meet sign-up deadlines, weather cancellations, race photos, and more.

💬 Join the WhatsApp Community

There are two groups: an announcements-only channel (coaches post, parents can reply) and an open group for broader conversation and photos.

3

Accept the Google Classroom invite

Once you've added your kid to the roster, the coaches will invite them to the team's Google Classroom. Your child just needs to accept the invite. Coaches post important info there so kids can stay independently informed: key updates are always cross-posted to WhatsApp too.
